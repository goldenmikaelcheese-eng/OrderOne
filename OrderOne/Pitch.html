01 — The problem

7 devices.
One job.
Zero sense.

Walk into any small or medium restaurant in the UAE doing delivery. On the counter you'll find a row of identical white devices — each from a different platform. They all do exactly the same thing: beep when an order arrives, show it on screen, and print a receipt staff staple to the bag.

Every platform insists on their own device. The store has no say. The counter is cluttered, staff juggle 7 screens, and drivers crowd inside because nobody knows whose order is ready.

It's a problem everyone sees. Nobody has fixed it — not in the UAE.

02 — The solution

One device.
Every platform.
Drivers stay outside.

01
One unified terminal
A single smart device replaces all 6–7 branded terminals. Every platform's orders arrive on one screen. One paper roll. One charger. One login.
02
Same workflow, less chaos
Nothing changes for staff. Order comes in, receipt prints, they staple it to the bag — except now it all comes from one device with the platform name clearly printed.
03
Outside pickup screen
A screen mounted outside shows drivers which order numbers are ready. They wait outside until their number appears. No more crowds at the counter.

What about drivers waiting for two orders?
Restaurant owners told us a real pain point — a driver gets two orders from the same restaurant. One ready in 5 minutes, the other in 15. The first sits getting cold. OrderOne solves this with smart split notifications no other system has.
