01 — The problem

7 devices.
One job.
Zero sense.

Walk into any restaurant in the UAE that does delivery. On the counter you'll find a row of identical white devices — each one from a different platform. They all do exactly the same thing: beep when an order arrives, show it on a small screen, and print a receipt the staff staple to the bag.

Every platform insists on their own device. The store has no say. The counter is cluttered, staff juggle 7 screens, and delivery drivers crowd inside because nobody knows whose order is ready yet.

It's a problem everyone sees. Nobody has fixed it — not in the UAE.

02 — The solution

One device.
Every platform.
Drivers stay outside.

01
One unified terminal
A single smart device replaces all 6–7 branded terminals. Every platform's orders arrive on one screen. Staff look at one device, use one paper roll, charge one thing.
02
Same workflow, less chaos
Nothing changes for staff. Order comes in, receipt prints, they staple it to the bag. Except now it all comes from one device — with the platform name clearly on each receipt.
03
Outside pickup screen
A screen mounted outside shows drivers which order numbers are ready. They wait outside until their number appears. No more crowds at the counter. No more going inside to ask.

06 — Business model

The platforms
pay us.

Right now, every platform buys, ships, and supports their own device for every single restaurant. That's expensive. OrderOne replaces their hardware — so they save money, and we charge them a fee per restaurant using our integration.
