The problem — from real restaurants in Dubai

Here is what we found.

Multiple tablets — constant chaos
Restaurants run a separate device for each platform. One for Talabat, one for Deliveroo, one for Noon. Staff miss orders because they cannot watch all screens at once during busy times.
Observed at: Pinkberry, Bartartine — Dubai
📦
Drivers arrive without packaging boxes
Pinkberry has a strict rule — drivers must bring their own packaging box to collect orders. If they arrive without one, Pinkberry refuses to give them the food. This causes arguments and delays at the pickup counter every single day.
Discovered at: Pinkberry — Dubai Hills Mall

Deliveroo device has no SIM card
The Deliveroo device relies entirely on mall WiFi. When WiFi drops the restaurant misses every single order with zero warning and zero backup.
Discovered at: Pinkberry — Dubai Hills Mall
🍹
Bar and restaurant orders mixed up
Restaurants with both food and drinks run two separate machines. Staff constantly confused about which counter an order belongs to. Orders get mixed up between bar and kitchen.
Observed at: Bartartine — Dubai
